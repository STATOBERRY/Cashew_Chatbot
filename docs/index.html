What is Cashew Chatbot?

Cashew Chatbot is an interactive assistant available on Telegram, designed to support cashew farmers, traders, and enthusiasts. It provides quick answers to farming queries, guidance on best agricultural practices, market price updates, and tips for increasing yield and quality.

Farmers can simply click the chat link or scan the QR code to start chatting instantly. The chatbot works 24/7, ensuring that help and information are always available, even during busy farming seasons.

How to use: Scan the QR code or click the Telegram link to connect. Ask any cashew-related question and receive instant answers.
